About me

I am an associated professor with the School of Computer and Information Engineering, Jiangxi Normal University, Nanchang, China. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Software and Theory from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, in 2014. My current research interests include evolutionary computation, swarm intelligence-based optimization algorithms, GPU-based parallel computing, and their applications to practical engineering projects. As the first or corresponding author, I have published more than 30 papers in international well-known journals or conferences in the fields of evolutionary optimization, such as Information Sciences, Applied Soft Computing, GECCO and CEC.

Email: xyzhou@jxnu.edu.cn OR xyzhou@whu.edu.cn
